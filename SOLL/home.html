Spring 2019 Registration

Spring Registration Opens November 10

$300 - League Age 8 & older (All kid pitch divisions - AA and up)
 $200 - League Age 7 & younger (Machine Pitch, Coach Pitch, and Tee Ball)

Things to know:

Registration is open November 10th until December 20th or until all spots are filled , whichever comes first. There are a limited number of spots so don't wait.

The Spring season begins in early February and goes through the end of May.

Tryouts are Jan 5th and 6th for kids that are League Age 7 and older (Machine Pitch and older). The makeup date will be the following weekend (January 12th & 13th).

Teams will be formed at the end of January, at which time you'll be contacted by amanager.

SOLL is a purely age-based league, and kids are placed on teams based on their 2019 "League Age" rather than skill or experience level. Your child's League Age is the age that they are as of August 31, 2019. For the 2019 Little League Age Chart, click here: /agechart

Managers will be selected in early December . If you're willing and able to manage your child's team or if you have any questions about what it entails, please email michaelross1818@me.com , and you can also sign up directly in volunteer signups when you register your child to play.

For any questions regarding registration or tryouts, please email azzie.mackenzie@fox.com.
